GOredirect tracks lost mobile devices, manage multiple account, also track sms. 
Note: Before  tracking of devices  or lost device, GOredirect must be installed in the two device, lets say Device A is the lost, and Device B is the tracking device. GOredirect must be installed in both the Lost Device  (Device A) and Tracker Device (Device B).
GOredirect helps to create account in MartWeb. Since martweb exist in 10  ways, GOredirect helps to create a single account for all the MartWeb (1-10).
After account is been created in GOredirect, User can easily sign in to MarWeb (1-10). Single account created in GOredirect can be use to signin to all the MartWeb. Dont create multiple Account in GOredirect.  You can not create account directly in MartWeb. MartWeb only allows you to sign in. 
Download GOredirect

You will need to purchase access key for activation of GOredirect. Once activated the full features of GOredirect will turn on.

This Key will also allow you to sign in to MartWeb (1-10).

Single Key can activate Both MartWeb and GOredirect. 

Key access for GOredirect is different from key access for PhonCash3x
