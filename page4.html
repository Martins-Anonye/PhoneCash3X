PhoneCash3x
PhoneCash3X production started  in  December 2019. Was first released in 27th september 2020 (during covid 19 pendemic). This first released was unstable, and series of versions where released during 2020.
Stable version was released in the middle of October 2021 at this period and Martweb(s) where also released, and everything where controlled by human.
Robotic version was release in February 2021, different series where released during this time. MartWeb and Robotic version of PhoneCash3x where not stable in performance.
The Boost version of PhoneCash3x was release on 3rd March 2022. This booster version has been stable and  remains the current type in-use. The boost version surport robotic controll and human controll. MartWeb(s) became stable in 4th of April 2022. Adzilyzer was first release in 14th August 2022 (available in playstore), The stable version of  Azilyzer was release in  16th October 2022. The purpose of Azilyzer is to make  of PhoneCash3x free and easy to use to the public.
PhoneCash3x is a Pay-to-use app.
